Meet the band

The Alice Blue Band
Chapel Hill, NC

We're Alice Blue, a local band in Chapel Hill, NC. We're a jazz and blues influenced band that loves jam out. We are made up five members, a lead singer and guitarist, a lead guitarist, a bassist, a pianist, and a drummer. Our lead singer is Noah Aiken, a sophmore at UNC. Our guitarist is Will Dominichi, a senior at UNC. Our bassist is Christopher Law, a junior at UNC. Our pianist is Drew Nora, a junior at UNC . Our drummer is Kyle Scheinder, an ECU alumni.

We love to play all types of events and are open to collaboration with other bands. Come check us out at some upcoming events. We will be live at Bowstring Brewery on Saturday, June 24th from 8:30pm-10pm in Chapel Hill, NC. For more booking information, check out our "Contact" page.
